# Stay Safe Online Checklist

## Things You Should Do
- Use strong passwords and try not to use the same one everywhere  
- Turn on two-factor authentication (it adds extra security)  
- Think before clicking on links or downloading anything  
- Double-check unknown emails, messages, or calls   
- Be careful when using public Wi-Fi (like in cafes or college)  

---

## Financial/UPI Safety
- Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone  
- Remember: you don’t need a PIN to receive money  
- Always check the name before making any payment  
- Avoid scanning random QR codes  

---

## If Something Goes Wrong
- Report it here: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call the helpline: **1930**
