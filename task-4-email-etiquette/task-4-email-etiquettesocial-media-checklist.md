# Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist

## Do's
- Be respectful and kind when posting or commenting  
- Think before you post anything online  
- Keep your profile clean and somewhat professional  
- Check your privacy settings regularly  
- Share useful or meaningful content  

---

## Don'ts
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly  
- Avoid posting anything offensive or inappropriate  
- Don’t believe or share everything you see online  
- Avoid unnecessary arguments or negativity  
- Don’t ignore your account security (like weak passwords)  

---
